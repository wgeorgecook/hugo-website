Some Notes⌗

As these are just the slides, you’re going to miss most of the content of the talk. But know that this is a very cursory introduction to Go. All of what I cover and more can be discovered on the Tour of Go. I wholly recommend you spend half an hour or so to complete that if you are interested in learning more about the Go syntax and features.
