Charity Yeti Charity Yeti aims to raise money for the Partner’s In Health maternal center of excellency in Sierra Leone. What began as a Tweet from Hank Green is growing to a platform that allows Twitter users to contribute to ending infant and mother mortality in Sierra Leone. Meet Needs Meet Needs is a community broker program that allows for basic moderation. Through Meet Needs, people can submit needs they need met with contact information that is only available to the moderator, and the need itself is posted to the community Needs Board.
